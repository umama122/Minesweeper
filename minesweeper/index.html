How to Play Minesweeper (Without Blowing Yourself Up)

  • It's a Minefield Out There: The game board is a grid full of cells. Some cells have bombs that will blast you to smithereens! Other cells are safe and cozy.
  • Choose Wisely Grasshopper: Click a cell to reveal it. If you don't see an earth-shattering KABOOM, woohoo! The number tells you how many bombs are lurking nearby. Keep clicking safe cells to rack up points.
  • Avoid the Hot Potatoes: Clicking a bomb ends the game real quick. You'll get slapped with a "Game Over" screen as punishment. Rude!
  • Mark Suspicious Cells: Right-click a cell to place a flag if you think it's a bomb. This helps jog your memory on places to avoid.
  • Mission: Don't Explode! Reveal all the safe cells while steering clear of bombs to win. Party like it's 1999 when you are victorious!

It takes some luck and logic, but you'll be a Minesweeper master in no time. Listen for sleigh bells ringing when you win, or prepare for the sad trombone if you detonate.

May the minesweep odds be ever in your favor! Now go bomb some bomblets!
